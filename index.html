Professional Values

I'm a museum technologist and digital humanist living and working in Bloomington, Indiana. I'm interested in ethical digital humanities and the ways that small interactions with digital artifacts reflect broader power dynamics in societies.

In the context of museums, that means that I'm interested in topics such as whether and why we open our collections to things like AI; prioritizing accessibility when/if adopting emerging tech like augmented and virtual reality; and imagining what collections data might look like if it respected and reflected cultural values beyond the limitations of a Western, cisgendered, and heteronormative perspective.

Imagining a future of museums built on ethical technology is the long game; my day-to-day work of supporting the museum's ongoing digital initiatives includes making tiny changes that will add up to meaningful change.
